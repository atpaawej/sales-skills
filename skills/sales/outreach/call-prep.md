# Call Prep Patterns

Loaded by `/outreach` when the founder needs to prep for a sales call.

---

## Steps

### 1. Gather prospect info

Ask:
- "Who is the prospect? (name, company, role)"
- "What do you already know about them?"
- "What's their relationship to you? (cold reply, inbound, trial user, referral?)"
- "What's your goal for this call? (qualify, demo, close?)"

Check the workspace — if this prospect already has a file in `.sales/calls/`, start with a status update.

### 2. Research the prospect

Use web search:
- Their LinkedIn profile — recent posts, activity, background
- Their company — what they do, recent news, funding, challenges
- Their role — what they care about, what metrics they're measured on
- Common ground — mutual connections, shared interests

If a browser tool is available (MCP), open their LinkedIn and company page for deeper reading.

### 3. Write the call flow

```
## Pre-Call Research
**Prospect:** [Name], [Role] at [Company]
**Company:** [what they do, size, relevant context]
**Triggers:** [what made them take this call]

## Call Flow

### 1. Rapport (2-3 min)
- Reference something specific: their recent post, company news, mutual connection

### 2. Discovery (15 min — 70% listening)
1. "What happened that made you take this call?"
2. "How is [problem] affecting you/your team today?"
3. "What have you tried so far? What didn't work?"
4. "If this problem disappeared, what would change?"
5. "Who else is involved? What do they care about?"
6. "What's your timeline? Budget range?"

### 3. Offer (5 min)
- Connect their specific situation to your solution
- "Based on what you told me, I think [specific outcome] is achievable."

### 4. Handle objections (5 min)
<pre-written responses for likely objections>

### 5. Next steps (2 min)
- If yes: set date and time immediately
- If no: ask what's missing
```

### 4. Pre-write objection scripts

Write 3-4 specific objections this prospect is likely to raise:

| Likely objection | Your response |
|---|---|
| "It's too expensive" | Acknowledge → Value reframe → "What would it cost you not to solve this?" |
| "We need to think about it" | "What specifically needs thinking through?" |
| "Happy with current solution" | "What would you improve if you could?" |
| "Not the right time" | "What needs to change for it to be the right time?" |
| "Send me some info" | "Happy to — what specifically are you evaluating?" |

Use Feel-Felt-Found: "I understand how you feel... Others have felt the same... What they found was..."

### 5. Write `.sales/calls/<prospect-name>.md`

Include prospect research, full call flow, discovery questions, and objection scripts.

---

## Completion criterion

Call prep file exists. Founder can review it in 5 minutes before the call. Discovery questions and objection scripts are specific to this prospect, not generic.
